About Me

Hobbies & Interests

I’m Heather—a front-end developer with a background as colorful as my cross-stitch projects and as adventurous as a game night with friends. My journey started in education, where I spent over a decade helping students overcome challenges and celebrating their growth. Today, I bring that same passion for problem-solving and creativity to building accessible and engaging digital experiences.

When I’m not coding or streaming, you’ll probably find me deep into a game of Magic: The Gathering, rolling dice in a D&D campaign, or modding my latest favorite video game. I also enjoy making tutorial videos on YouTube, sharing what I’ve learned with others. Away from the screen, I love curling up with a good book—usually with one of my six cats keeping me company (three of whom I just couldn’t resist adopting as foster fails).

My cats are constant reminders to stay curious, playful, and a little chaotic—qualities I happily bring into my work and hobbies alike. In every project I take on, whether building a website or crafting a new story for my D&D group, I find joy in creating experiences that bring people together.
