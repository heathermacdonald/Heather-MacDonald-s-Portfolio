My diverse background sets me apart as a developer who values creativity, inclusivity, and accessibility. I am passionate about using my technical skills and educational expertise to solve complex problems and design web experiences that welcome every user. I am excited to bring my unique perspective to a forward-thinking team that values accessibility, innovation, and inclusive design.
